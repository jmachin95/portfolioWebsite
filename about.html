About Me


I am a Computer Science student at Keele University, aspiring to become a front-end web developer. My plans are to improve my HTML, CSS and JavaScript skills to later learn JavaScript frameworks such as React.

My passion for programming started in 2015 when we used Scratch to make small, little, simple programs in our I.T lessons at school. I would come home and start to create little platformer games and work on them throughout the whole day. When we got a new laptop this was the first program I installed until I began to learn more languages.

Outside of my studies and my coding ventures, I love to play football and follow my favourite football team - Stoke City. I watch most games they play and I have done this ever since I was very young. There have been some amazing times as well as hard and frustrating times.
